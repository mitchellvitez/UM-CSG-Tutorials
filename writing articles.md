# Writing an Article for the CSG Website

To write an article, you need "Author" or "Editor" permissions. If you're not sure what these mean or you need to escalate your permissions, please contact the [CSG Webmaster](mailto:csg.webmaster@umich.edu)

## Getting to the Edit page

First, log in to the CSG site. Click `Add Content` in the bar at the top

Click `Article`

## Writing

Write your article! You can add images, links, whatever you want.

If you want to, edit the `Authoring Information` to use your real name instead of your uniqname.
